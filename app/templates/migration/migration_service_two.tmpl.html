Pre-migration Assessment Report

Report of pre-migration assessment. Use this information to address flagged issues with your ASE

Table Transfer Method
Purpose: A breakdown of table migration methods
Summary: The Migration Service automatically analyses the table data to be migrated and determines the best method of transferring that table
Data Transfer Volume
Purpose: Percentage of data being transferred in a particular method
Summary: The migration service automatically analyses data volumes and types then determines the most appropriate method of migrating that data
Data Compression(%)
Purpose: Percentage of data available for compression during the migration
Summary: Informs the customer how much data could be compressed as part of the migration. The customer can then look at 'Migration Compression Progression' figures to decide how much of that percentage they actually want to compress during the migration.
